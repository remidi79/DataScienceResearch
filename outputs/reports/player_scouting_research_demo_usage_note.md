# Player Scouting Research Demo Usage Note

Experiment 015 may be used safely only as an internal research/demo artefact.

## Allowed

- Internal research review.
- Methodology discussion.
- UI prototype with clear research-only badges.
- Metric/dimension validation.
- Analyst feedback.

## Not allowed

- Production ranking.
- Recruitment decision automation.
- Provider-direct replacement.
- Public claim of production scouting score.
- Comparison against paid provider models as equivalent.

## Required badges / wording

Use all of the following when displaying Experiment 015 outputs:

- Research only.
- Event-derived only.
- Not provider-direct.
- Not production-ready.
- Low sample size: 11 matches, 1 competition, 1 season.
- Similarity is same-role only and low-sample flagged.
- Does not replace licensed provider-direct scouting scores.

## Safe interpretation

Experiment 015 demonstrates whether the role/dimension/scoring mechanics are understandable and useful for analyst review. It does not validate production scouting quality, recruitment decisions, or paid-provider equivalence.
