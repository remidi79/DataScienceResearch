# Score Versioning

- 0.1 Prototype: early research artefacts, no production use.
- 0.5 Research Validated: Experiments 001-007 architecture complete, local/sample validation only.
- 0.9 Full Population Validated: complete StatsBomb multi-season/multi-competition recalibration and validation passed.
- 1.0 Production Ready: expert review complete, production gates passed, immutable coefficient bundle signed and documented.

Current version: 0.5.0-research-validated.

Every version must store source dataset snapshot, experiment outputs, config hash, coefficient bundle, and validation report.
